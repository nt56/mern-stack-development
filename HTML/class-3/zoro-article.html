About

Roronoa Zoro, also known as "Pirate Hunter Zoro," is the first crewmate to join Monkey D. Luffy in One Piece. Created by Eiichiro Oda, Zoro is the swordsman of the Straw Hat Pirates and dreams of becoming the world’s greatest swordsman, a title currently held by Dracule Mihawk. Hailing from the East Blue, Zoro's unwavering loyalty, stoic demeanor, and incredible combat skills make him one of the most respected and feared characters in the pirate world.
